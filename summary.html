My Favorite Movie

Humanity is already on the brink of extinction due to climate change and lack of food when a mysterious rift in the fabric of space-time is discovered that promises a chance for survival.
Former NASA pilot Cooper is sent with a team of researchers on a mission through the wormhole to another solar system to search for new habitats.
It's a journey into the unknown, because wormholes have hardly been explored, and no one knows what awaits on the other side.
